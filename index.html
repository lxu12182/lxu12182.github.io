FAQs

1. What time does the ceremony start?

Please be seated and ready for a 4pm start on the day :)

2. When do I need to RSVP by?

We kindly ask that all RSVPs be completed by the Monday 15th September to allow ample time for meal and seating arrangements

3. What is the dress code?

The celebration dress code is cocktail attire.

4. Can I bring a plus one?

We are hoping to celebrate our special day with those closest to us. As such, invitations are solely for those named on the invite.

5. Can I bring my child(ren)?

To allow all guests to relax and enjoy themselves, we have chosen to make our special day adults-only.

6. Is there parking at the venue?

Unfortunately, there is no on-site parking at the venue. There is a limited amount of on-street parking (costs may apply) and a paid car park around the corner (Wilson Parking Morningside).

We strongly recommend carpooling, or using public transport/uber to travel to and from the venue.

7. Am I allowed to buy gifts not in the registry?

We love and appreciate the thought that you are putting into your gift purchase, however, we would prefer gifts to be purchased using the registry as a guide to prevent double-ups on items that we already have 🤍

Gift items do not have to be the exact model/brand shown on the registry - any similar alternatives will be loved just as much!

Please also remember to tick off your gift purchase on the registry once this has been completed to prevent duplicates.
